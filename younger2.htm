Younger Actors

If you are looking for a camp that has a performing arts emphasis, but also offers other activities, check out the listings at the American Camp Association site http://find.acacamps.org/finding_advanced.php. There is a form to fill out where you can indicate areas of interest, location, special needs, etc. The search will return those camps that fit the criteria you've specified.
