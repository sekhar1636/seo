How To Contact Us!

If you need to reach us the week of the auditions, please use the email address below. Do not contact Pace University for information about auditions or accommodations.

If the website has not answered your questions, please email us at info@ strawhat-auditions.com.

In case you have problems getting to our website, or sending sending emails to StrawHat, please email us at jcspadone@gmail.com.

If you have an emergency or have a problem that can only be solved by phone, call 203-254-8572, but email is the preferred method of contact. Please do not call us about the status of your application.

Notification of Registration, Audition:
You will receive notification of your registration by mail and email once your complete application has been received and processed. If you are scheduled for an audition you will also receive notification of your audition status by mail and email. Mailed audition notification will confirm all the details of the audition. If you are not selected for an audition, the mailed notice will include your returned audition fee.

To mail your application, portfolio or other material, please use the address below:
