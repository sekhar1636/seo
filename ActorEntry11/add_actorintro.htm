Welcome to StrawHat On-Line Registration. StrawHat registration services are open to anyone interested in a career in the performing arts. Auditions are restricted to non-Equity performers 18 years of age or older.

PLEASE FOLLOW THESE INSTRUCTIONS:

  • You must complete all three of the numbered steps below to be considered for an audition appointment.
  • IMPORTANT! Make sure your print font on your resume is large enough to read in a dark theatre!
  • Do not enter more than one registration!
  • Refer to Audition Types to determine what you want to apply for.
  • Make sure your First and Last Name entries match your name as it appears on your resume.
  • Be sure to register with an email address that will not be blocked by your server. We use your email to notify you of audition information, changes to your registration, confirmation of your audition status and more. If you are not sure, ask your administrator.
  • Use the CHANGE ACTOR APPLICATION link to make changes, complete and printout your application.
  • Refer to our Resume Review and Headshot Review sections on the previous page for tips and suggestions.
  • If you have problems, refer to the FAQ's. Contact us at info@strawhat-auditions.com if problems persist. for help. Include your name and your User ID number in your email.
  • IF YOU APPLIED LAST YEAR, please note that your application is removed for security purposes after the summer season; so you will have to do a new application for this year.

STEP 1:

  • COMPLETE the on-line application to create your own On-Line Casting Profile.
  • PRINT out two copies of the on-line application: one to mail in with your application and one for your records. The printout includes your actor ID number.

STEP 2: EMAIL us your headshot and resume (Subject Line should read Last Name, First Name, UID Number)

  • Your headshot must be a jpg file only.
  • Your one-page resume must be pdf file only.
  • Send both as attachments to an email to info@strawhat-auditions.com with the following in the Subject Line: Last Name, First Name, and UID number (found in the last line of the header on your application print out).

STEP 3: MAIL us the following items in one large envelope:

  • Your application printout, signed and dated.
  • Two (2) self-addressed business-size (#10) envelopes with correct postage. (see our FAQs) These are used to confirm (1) receipt of your application and (2) to confirm acceptance to the auditions. Insufficient postage will result in processing delays. Please use a permanent home address as the return address on your envelopes.
  • Your standard 8x10 headshot with your one-page resume attached, back to back. Color or Black/White are acceptable. Crop your resume to match the 8x10 format. Do not submit resumes with hand-written additions or corrections.
  • Your $40 registration fee* (money order or check) payable to StrawHat Auditions. This fee is nonrefundable.
  • Your $55 audition fee* (a SEPARATE check or money order) payable to StrawHat Auditions. This check will be returned if you are not scheduled for an appointment; but you will have access to the information provided on our website, and your picture and resume profile will remain published online.
  • Please be sure that YOUR NAME appears on your checks. You can write it in the memo line if the account is not yours.
  • Mail the complete package to: StrawHat Auditions, 1771 Post Road East, #315, Westport, CT 06880.

*There is a minimum $25 service charge for any check returned unpaid by your bank.

PLEASE NOTE:

  • Incomplete applications will not be processed.
  • Registration does not guarantee an audition appointment.
  • No refunds for cancellations or non-appearance.
  • Stand-by slots are available, but are strictly limited and scheduled in advance.
  • Refer to FAQ's.
  • No replacements or walk-ins.
